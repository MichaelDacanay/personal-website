March 1, 2020

What a ride it has been these past few weeks! I am fortunate and grateful to God for giving me the opportunity to become a Caldwell Fellow. It is an honor but also an exciting possibility of new friendships and mentors. This past weekend, I traveled with the other classmates in the Class of 2023 to a retreat in Greensboro where we focused on building community and deeper relationships. Lately, I have been reminding myself to remember to remain humble, ambitious, and hardworking. I have been trying to focus on what matters most in my life, organizing my priorities, and being efficient. A lesson learned from my conversations with others is an attitude of gratitude and service and not taking things for granted. Our time on earth is short and quick-lived; do not live life in such a way that you will regret looking backward. As exams and projects come up before the spring break, keep up the persistence and hard work. Good night!
